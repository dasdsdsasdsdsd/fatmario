Skyline Ops

Original 3D FPS prototype with training range objectives.

Health: 100 Ammo: 30 Targets: 0 / 8

Training Briefing

Lock the cursor, clear the holographic targets, and stay inside the range walls. This is an original 3D FPS training experience.

Move: WASD Look: Mouse Sprint: Shift Fire: Left click Reload: R Pause: Esc

Objective

  • Eliminate all 8 targets in the range.
  • Targets respawn after a short delay.
  • Maintain ammo and reload when needed.
